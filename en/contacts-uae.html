Frequently Asked Questions

🎒 What to Bring?

Swimsuit/trunks, towel, sunscreen, sunglasses, change of clothes. All diving equipment, water, snacks, and meals we provide. If you have your own dive computer or mask — bring it along.

🏥 Medical Requirements?

You need to fill out a PADI medical form. If you have chronic conditions (asthma, diabetes, heart problems, epilepsy), a doctor's note is required. No diving under alcohol influence or with cold/flu.

👶 Minimum Age?

For discovery dive (DSD) and snorkeling — from 10 years. For PADI Open Water course — from 10 (Junior OW) or from 15 (full OW). For fun diving — PADI/SSI certificate or equivalent required, minimum 10 years.

❌ Cancellation Policy?

Free cancellation 24 hours before the trip. Same-day cancellation — possible in force majeure (illness, bad weather). We always accommodate and offer rescheduling. Bad weather — reschedule at no extra cost.

💰 How to Pay?

Payment on-site on the diving day. We accept cash in dirhams (AED), Visa/Mastercard bank cards. No prepayment required — just confirm your booking.

🤿 Do I Need a Certificate?

For fun diving — yes, a valid certificate (PADI, SSI, CMAS, etc.) and logbook are required. For discovery dive (DSD) — no certificate needed, any healthy person from age 10 can dive.

🌊 Is Diving Safe?

Safety is our priority. All instructors are PADI certified. Equipment undergoes regular maintenance. Insurance included. Small groups (max 4 divers per instructor). First aid kit and oxygen always on board.

✈️ Diving and Flying?

After one dive, wait 12 hours before flying. After multiple dives in a day — minimum 18-24 hours. Plan your diving not on the last day before your flight.
