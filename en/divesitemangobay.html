Mango Bay Dive Site

A shallow dive site in one of Koh Tao's bays, ideal for Open Water Diver level. Covered with huge corals, especially the western part. You can drop off before entering the bay for a deeper dive. Wildlife: blue-spotted stingray, grey ray, titan triggerfish, fusilier, parrotfish, seahorse.
