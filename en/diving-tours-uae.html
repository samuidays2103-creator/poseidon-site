Diving Seasons in UAE

Diving is available year-round. Water never drops below 22°C. However, conditions vary by season.

Best Season

October — May

Best visibility up to 15–20 meters. Comfortable water temperature 24–27°C. Calm sea. Ideal conditions for training and deep dives. High season — book in advance.

June — September

Warm water 28–30°C. Visibility 5–10 meters. More plankton — more large fish: rays, sharks, turtles. Low season — best prices and fewer people at dive sites.
