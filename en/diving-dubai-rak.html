How a Diving Day Goes

A fully organized day — from your hotel door to the underwater world and back

06:00

Hotel Pickup

We pick you up from your hotel early morning. Dubai departure around 06:00, Sharjah — 06:15, RAK — 07:00. Comfortable air-conditioned vehicle.

08:00

Arrival in Fujairah

Arrive at the dive center. Equipment preparation, dive site briefing, gear check. Tea, coffee, light snack before dives.

09:00

First Dive

Head out by boat to the dive site. First dive for 40-60 minutes. Coral reefs, sea turtles, reef sharks, tropical fish.

11:00

Surface Interval & Lunch

Break between dives. Lunch at a seaside restaurant. Rest, discuss what you saw, prepare for the second dive.

13:00

Second Dive

Second dive at a different site. Diverse underwater landscapes — from coral gardens to underwater rocks and sunken objects.

15:00

Return Home

Return equipment, dry off, share impressions. Drive back. In Dubai by 17:00-17:30, in RAK by 16:30. Receive photos and videos.

What You Need to Know

🎒 What to Bring

Swimsuit, towel, sunscreen, change of clothes. Everything else — equipment, water, snacks — we provide. If you have your own mask or computer — bring it along.

🛣 About the Drive

The road from Dubai to Fujairah is one of the most beautiful in UAE. We drive through the Hajar Mountains with stunning views. Excellent road, air conditioning, music. Time flies.

👨‍👩‍👧 For the Whole Family

While you dive, your family can relax at Sandy Beach near Snoopy Island. Snorkeling, swimming, cafes. Or take the whole family for a discovery dive — from age 10.
