Diving in Dubai & Fujairah

Dubai is the perfect starting point for diving in the UAE. Just 1.5 hours in a comfortable minibus — and you're on the Indian Ocean coast in Fujairah, where coral reefs, tropical fish and crystal clear water await you.

Transfer Logistics

We pick you up right from your Dubai hotel early in the morning. The drive takes about 1.5 hours along the scenic road through the Hajar Mountains. After the dives, we bring you back by evening.

Drive ~1.5h

What Makes Dubai-Based Diving Special

Indian Ocean

Fujairah's coast is the only place in the UAE with access to the Indian Ocean. The underwater world here is completely different from the Persian Gulf: richer corals, more large fish, better visibility.

Warm Water Year-Round

Water temperature from 24°C in winter to 30°C in summer. Comfortable dives without a thick wetsuit. A thin 3mm suit or shorty — all you need most of the year.

Convenient for Dubai Residents

A full day of adventure with return home by dinner. No need to book a hotel, take vacation or fly somewhere. Perfect option for a weekend or vacationers in Dubai.

Fujairah Marine Life

Fujairah's coast is famous for its incredible marine fauna diversity. Here's what you can encounter on your dives:
